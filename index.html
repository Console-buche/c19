COVID-19 - QUARANTINE AWARENESS - EXPERIMENT

This is a simulation of a few city blocks, with a public park and a supermarket. People, represented by small green sphere, go about their daily lives : waking up, going to work, for a run in the park or shopping.
The aim of this simulation is to raise awareness and allow people to experience how impactful and necessary quarantine may be to fight the infection.
Adjust the behaviour values in the top-right menu and start the simulation.
